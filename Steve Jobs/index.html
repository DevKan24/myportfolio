Steve Jobs

The man who changed the Tech Industry

A brief timeline of Steve Jobs:

  • 1955 - Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976 - Co-founded Apple to create personal computers.
  • 1984 - Launched the Macintosh, a revolutionary computer.
  • 1985 - Left Apple and started NeXT.
  • 1986 - Bought Pixar, which later became famous for animated films.
  • 1996 - Apple bought NeXT, bringing Jobs back.
  • 1997 - Jobs became Apple’s CEO again, leading the company’s comeback.
  • 2001 - Launched the iPod, changing music forever.
  • 2007 - Released the iPhone, transforming mobile technology.
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passed away, leaving a lasting legacy.

"Steve Jobs was the greatest business executive of our time. He was the master of innovation and execution. He changed the world in ways that most of us can only dream of."

– Tim Cook, CEO of Apple
